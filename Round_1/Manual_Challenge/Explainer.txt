The true price of this good is 30 as we can resell it later for it. Our goal is therefore to either buy it for less of sell for more.
Here we notice that there is an ask order for 28 (<30) and this is what we will use to mke our PnL.
We will try every possibility to find he one that will get us as much PnL as possible, the order volume will be denoted by V and the bid price by P :
For P >= 30 and V >= 10k, PnL <= 0
For P >= 30 and V < 10k, PnL = V as the clearance price will be 29 netting us a PnL/unit of 1. The maximum possible PnL using this technique is therefore 9999.
For P = 29 and V >= 5k, the PnL will be 5k for the same reason as above
For P = 29 and V < 5k, the PnL will be 2V as the clearance price will be 28 netting us a PnL/unit of 2. The maximum possible PnL using this technique is therefore 9998.
For P <= 28, we are completely excluded from the trade so no PnL is realized.
Hence, the best action for us is a bid of P >= 30 and V = 9999

Ember mushroom cf embermushroom.jpeg:
This is going to be a generalization of the previous technique.
For a given price P, we will take the maximum of orders M we can take so that we max out the ask volume of P - 1. Then placing a bid order of M - 1 at price P will allow us to get M - 1 units at price P - 1.
I believe this is optimal as at any price, another order with V >= M will trigger a clearance price of P which will reduce our PnL (unless exceptional case where the ask(P-1) is very low suddendly, which is not the case with ember mushroom) and V < M - 1 is losing out on PnL. 
There are edge cases where dropping V more allows P to go even lower and allowing more PnL but there are none in this case.
Hence, the best action for us is a bid of P >= 17 and V = 19999
